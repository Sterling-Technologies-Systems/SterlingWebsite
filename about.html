Sterling Technologies & Systems Limited is a Nigerian Information Communication Technology Company in partnership with various organizations, to provide unique solution to clients in various sectors of the economy.

Sterling Technologies & Systems was incorporated in 2001 to actively play in the emerging IT and e-business market and become the dominant IT and e-Business Services Provider company in Nigeria and indeed Africa. Our board members, act in a directory capacity drawing on their wealth of experience and expertise in Communication, Information Technology, Banking & Finance, Payment System and e-Business Development.
